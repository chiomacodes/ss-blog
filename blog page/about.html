About Me

"Good company in a journey makes the way seem shorter." - Izaak Walton

Staying Stylish was originially founded on December 1st 2019 with the aim of creating posts dedicated to self-care, self-improvement and think pieces. It was also created as a literary platform to promote my Depop and gift others with my (at the time) overflowing wardrobe collection. I've always adored writing and wanted to share it with whoever deemed it worth reading. As I grow older, I hope I can still contribute meaningful information and interesting topics of discussion for years to come! Enjoy :)
